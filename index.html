B.Tech Journey

Discover the semester-wise progression of the B.Tech program, designed to build your engineering expertise.

Books

Semester 1

Introduction to Engineering, Mathematics I, Physics, Manufacturing processes

Semester 2

Mathematics II, Chemistry, Data Structures, Engineering Graphics

Semester 3

Strength of Materials I, Engineering Geology, Surveying I, Fluid Mechanics I

Semester 4

Structural Analysis I, Building Materials & Construction, Surveying II, Fluid Mechanics II

Semester 5

Concrete Technology, Geotechnical Engineering I, Transportation Engineering I, Design of Steel Structures

Semester 6

Structural Analysis II, Geotechnical Engineering II, Water Resources Engineering, Transportation Engineering II

Semester 7

Environmental Engineering, Construction Management & Estimation, Elective I (e.g., Remote Sensing / GIS), Project Work / Internship

Semester 8

Capstone Project / Advanced Structural Design, Elective II (e.g., Earthquake Engineering), Professional Ethics & Safety, Seminar
